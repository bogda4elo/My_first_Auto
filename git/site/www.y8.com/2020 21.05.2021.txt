2020 Game is a fun game about the mad year of 2020. A year that contained so many world-changing, paradigm-shifting developments running every possible scenario at once. In this game, play through all the major events of 2020 such as The Australia Wildfires, Covid-19, The Stock Market crash, Quarantine, The rise of Tiktok and the US Elections all those scenarios in this game. Have fun and enjoy playing this game here at Y8.com!
