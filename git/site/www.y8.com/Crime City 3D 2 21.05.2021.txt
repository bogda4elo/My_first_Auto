In this third-person shooter action game, you play as an agent who's aim is to clear y8 city from crime.
Take your revenge on the mafia guys in your city.
Shoot them down.
Cool and long lasting more than 150 missions.
You can steal any vehicle you wish - auto, car, tank, helicopter, jet pack. using Enter key near the vehicle. The game contains fully Open world Environment.
Buy and upgrade your weapons to destroy the enemies and clear missions.

Use cars and vehicles to drive over them.
