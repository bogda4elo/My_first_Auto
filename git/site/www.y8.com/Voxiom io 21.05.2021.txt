Have you ever wanted to play a game with incredibly huge terrain and with lots of online players around? Well, Voxiom IO is what you need then. Simply enter the battle ground and switch between items in your inventory. You will also find similar mechanics here, such as crafting or drawing cubes. This way you can create various weapons, thanks to which you can then better defend against enemies. Or you can build a small house and use it as a shield. As this is a battle royale, your goal will be to stay alive as long as possible. When all the enemies are dead, you win. Let's do this! Play this exciting game only on y8.com
