Yummy Toast a fun and excited cooking game. Our cute little bear wants to cook toasts and serve all his friends. So for that he need our help to cook and serve. There are some important tasks to perform to make yummy toasts. First, let us clean the stove and the frying pan with some cleaners, just follow the steps and collect the bread, taco, cheese, and some other sauces and fry them and be prepared to serve for customers. Finally, help our cute little bear to serve the toasts by the order done from the customers. Place taco, cheese, bread, and other toppings as per the customer's order. Serve 10 orders to win the game. Have fun playing this game only on y8.com
