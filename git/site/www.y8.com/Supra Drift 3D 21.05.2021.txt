Supra Drift 3D is a great opportunity to take spin in the famous Toyota Supra. In this game you won't only have the freedom to drive around, but also bring a personal touch to it. You can first pick a color, choose a cool body-kit and then go tune it! This beast of a machine is good for both racing and drifting. With a realistic city environment waiting for you, you'll get the chance to burn some rubber anywhere you want. No annoying traffic or chance that you run out of fuel. Just have fun!
